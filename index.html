Swimming

Take a pause and swim away from your worries. Let Domescape's natural swimming pools soothe your senses.

Bonfire

The draw of a hot crackling fire is hard to resist for adults and children alike. What could be more evocative than the thought of sitting around a blazing fire, late into the evening, toasting marshmallows, chatting, throwing on the occasional log and listening to the owls hooting close by, the stars twinkling above.

About Us

DOMESCAPE is the first of its kind in the Philippines. A family vacation domes that we decided to share whenever not in use. We want our guests to experience raw nature w/o having to sacrifice comfort. Our geodesic dome is more than just a tent, we want our guests to experience the comfort of staying in a luxury room with the great outdoors as its lobby. Most parts of the property were left untouched to provide a truly authentic experience. Domescape is for adventure seekers and nature lover.
