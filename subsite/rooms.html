Dome 1

Base Rate is good for 4 persons
Allowed Extra Persons: 4 with Php1,000 fee for each person
Domescape is the first dome glamping in the Philippines. We are located in Bgy. Tumalim, Nasugbu Batangas. It is a family project vacation domes that we decided to share and rent out whenever not in use.
Our dome is fully air-conditioned and is equipped with mini-fridge, kettle heater, microwave oven, and humidifier fan.
Guests will have their private natural swimming pool beside their dome. Common area with bonfire at night may be used by guests.

Amenities

• 2 Twin Beds (good for 2 persons each)
• Outdoor Bathroom (cold water only)
• Outdoor dining
• Outdoor Kitchen
• Natural Swimming Pool

About Us

DOMESCAPE is the first of its kind in the Philippines. A family vacation domes that we decided to share whenever not in use. We want our guests to experience raw nature w/o having to sacrifice comfort. Our geodesic dome is more than just a tent, we want our guests to experience the comfort of staying in a luxury room with the great outdoors as its lobby. Most parts of the property were left untouched to provide a truly authentic experience. Domescape is for adventure seekers and nature lover.
